Our Story
IFA Mortgages & Finance was founded in 2001 by Anthony O’Flynn & Michael Hanratty. The Company has been operating successfully for the past 16 years and has provided service to thousands of satisfied clients in this time.

Our mission is to guarantee that any client who seeks our assistance is guided seamlessly through the processes involved with obtaining finance, ensuring that their financial goals are realised in a stress-free manner. We strive to communicate actively with our clients from pre-submission stage through to and beyond settlement, working towards a mutual goal. We endeavour to provide ongoing service & maintain a strong relationship with our clients, and empower them with the knowledge and confidence necessary to move forward and reach their next financial goal.
The complexities and struggles of obtaining a mortgage can be a daunting task. Luckily, we can take the stress and difficulty out of securing the best possible mortgage for your situation. Not only will we provide the best strategy possible for your respective loan while saving you money and time. Based out of Bondi Junction, we also provide our mobile service state-wide by appointment, with our expert mortgage brokers highly adept in simple to complex lending.
